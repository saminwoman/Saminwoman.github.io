Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

Delivery times vary depending on the store and shipping method, but most orders arrive within 10-14 business days after processing. Express shipping options (5-7 days) are available for an additional fee.

You can shop from hundreds of international stores including Amazon, Shein, ASOS, Nike, Adidas, Sephora, and many more. If you don't see your preferred store listed, just contact our support team and we'll check if we can accommodate your request.

We use live exchange rates from trusted sources like NRXB and Tether to calculate the IRR equivalent of USD prices. Our system updates these rates multiple times daily to ensure accuracy. The final price includes the product cost, international shipping, and our service fee.

We accept payments through ZarinPal, NextPay, and bank transfers. Installment options are available through Saman Bank and Pasargad Bank. All payments are secure and encrypted.

We offer a 14-day return policy for eligible items. Please contact our support team within 3 days of receiving your package to initiate a return. Return shipping fees may apply depending on the reason for return. Refunds will be processed once we receive the returned item.
